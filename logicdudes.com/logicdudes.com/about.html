About LogicDudes

Originally registered as a fictitious name in 2011, LogicDudes is now an LLC co owned by Kevin Ashcraft and Kaitlyn Powell. We are starting our web design business in our home town because our home town needs to be modern. Auburndale is growing, Poly Tech is coming, and we want to be apart of the experience! LogicDudes aims to modernize local businesses by crafting their Internet presence and creating powerful web applications.

Kevin Ashcraft, RHCSA

Linux Administrator

Kevin is our Linux Administrator and script writer, coding most of the PHP and Javascript used to power our sites. He was born and raised in Auburndale, graduated from AHS in 2004, and has spent the past ten years providing tech support, maintaining small business networks, and discovering what it means to be a modern business.
